Frequently Asked

Questions, answered — just message us if you need more

Minimum age?
Renters must be 21 or older with a valid driver’s license and insurance. Some vehicles require renters to be 25+.
Do you deliver?
Yes — island-wide delivery in Puerto Rico: your hotel, the airport, or your front door. A delivery fee applies depending on your location.
How do I book?
Reserve online or message us — the fastest way. We confirm availability and arrange delivery.
Deposit required?
Yes, a refundable security deposit starting at $750. Higher deposits apply on select high-value vehicles.
Mileage limits?
Mileage allowance varies by vehicle — we’ll confirm your car’s daily limit when you book.
Chauffeur option?
Yes! Professional chauffeur service from $125/hour in a Cadillac Escalade — perfect for events and nights out. 2-hour minimum in the metro area (4-hour minimum outside the metro area), and airport/point-to-point transfers are available too.
Weekly & monthly rates?
Weekly rates are shown on each vehicle. Monthly rates are available on request — just ask.
Cancellation policy?
If your rental was paid in full, we refund up to 50%. If only a deposit was paid to reserve the dates, it’s non-refundable — unless the deposit exceeds 50% of the rental value.
