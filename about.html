About Us

Puerto Rico's home for the extraordinary drive

Palm Exotic Rentals was built on one belief: an unforgettable trip deserves an unforgettable drive. We put the keys to the island's finest exotics and premium SUVs in your hands — delivered to your door, wrapped in service that feels personal from the first message to the final drop-off.

Our Story

How It Started

Palm Exotic Rentals began in San Juan out of a genuine love for exceptional cars — and the feeling of driving one for the first time. What started as a small, hand-picked collection of exotics and luxury SUVs grew quickly, powered by word of mouth and a reputation for five-star service.

From day one, the mission was simple: make renting something extraordinary feel effortless. Today we deliver island-wide across Puerto Rico, offer professional chauffeur service, and we're growing — with Miami and a curated villa collection on the way.

Our Vision

Where We're Headed

To become the Caribbean's most trusted name in luxury mobility — the first call for visitors and locals who want to experience Puerto Rico behind the wheel of something special. We're building more than a rental service; we're building the way people remember their time on the island.

Our Goal

What Drives Us

To make luxury effortless. Whether it's the keys to a Lamborghini for the weekend, a chauffeured Escalade for the evening, or a villa to match, our goal never changes: deliver the experience straight to you — clean, on time, and exactly as promised.
